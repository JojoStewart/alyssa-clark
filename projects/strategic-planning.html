Strategy

Equity-Centered Strategic Planning

Promise Partnership Utah · Salt Lake City · 2021–2024

Overview

Led an equity-centered 5-year strategic planning process for Promise Partnership Utah, a cradle-to-career collective impact partnership working to close racial and economic disparities in the greater Salt Lake region. The process integrated data, funding, strategies, and partnership priorities into a unified roadmap.

Approach

The strategic plan required facilitation across multiple organisational departments to ensure alignment in strategies, measurement, and evaluation. I worked as a strategic adviser to senior leadership and cross-sector partnerships, holding both technical rigour and relationships as priorities throughout the process.

Key activities included:

  • Facilitating alignment sessions across organisational departments and external partners
  • Integrating data strategy, funding priorities, and partnership goals into a cohesive plan
  • Developing measurement frameworks centered on racial and economic disparities in education, health, and financial systems
  • Building operational systems to ensure the longevity of strategies beyond the planning period

Outcomes

  • Delivered a clear 5-year roadmap with measurable milestones adopted across the partnership
  • Increased team capacity for evaluation through training and documentation
  • Established operational systems ensuring continuity of strategies and processes
  • Embedded racial equity as a structural priority across all strategic goals
