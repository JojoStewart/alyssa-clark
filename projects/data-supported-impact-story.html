Data Supported Impact Story

Promise Partnership Utah · 2026

Overview

Developed a set of compelling, data-supported impact stories to demonstrate the effectiveness and scale of Promise Partnership Utah's place-based collective impact work. These stories were crafted for multiple audiences to illustrate the connection between outcomes and strategies, partnerships, and conditions that contributed to and/or made those improvements possible.

Approach

I identifed, analyzed, and synthesized the most compelling outcomes improvements achieved at the greatest scale, then translated them into clear, persuasive narratives supported by high-quality data visualizations.

Key activities included:

  • Analyzing longitidunal data to identify significant trends and outcomes improvements
  • Reviewing and synthesize historical organizational reports and documentation
  • Constructing strategic narrative and identified key data impact stories for key audiences
  • Developing communication materials for multiple audiences in multiple formats
  • Creating clear documentation of analysis processes embedded in organization's existing documentation structures

Outcomes

  • Data-supported impact stories with clear descriptions of the outcome improvement, data evidence, and brief narratives
  • One-pagers with data and narrative supporting outcome improvements
  • A comprehensive PowerPoint deck with data visualizations and supporting narrative
  • Clear and complete data sourcing and data notes for each story
  • Compelling, publication-ready data visualizations for each story
