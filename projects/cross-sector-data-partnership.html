Research

Cross-Sector Data Sharing Partnership

University of Pennsylvania · Equity in Practice Learning Community · 2023–2024

Overview

Led project development and stakeholder coordination for a cross-sector data sharing partnership as part of the University of Pennsylvania's Equity in Practice Learning Community. The project brought together partners from different sectors to build community-centered data governance and sharing models.

Approach

  • Managed partnership agreements and data governance protocols across participating organisations
  • Designed community engagement processes to ensure data sharing reflected community priorities
  • Developed and presented a project proposal demonstrating community-centered partnership models
  • Coordinated across partners with different institutional contexts, timelines, and data systems

Outcomes

  • Secured $50,000 grant to fund the partnership's implementation
  • Established data governance protocols centered on community consent and benefit
  • Built a replicable model for cross-sector data sharing partnerships
