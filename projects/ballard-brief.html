Research Communication

Researcher & Writer (Ballard Brief)

Ballard Center for Social Impact, BYU · Provo, UT · 2017–2018

Overview

As a Ballard Brief writer and editor at BYU's Ballard Center for Social Impact, researched and published two articles examining pressing social issues: illiteracy among adults in the United States and mental illness among refugees in the Middle East. Each article drew on 200+ hours of research to describe the issue, assess existing interventions, and identify gaps.

Approach

  • Conducted extensive literature reviews spanning academic research, policy documents, and practitioner reports
  • Assessed existing practices and interventions addressing each social issue
  • Synthesized findings into accessible, evidence-based publications for a broad audience
  • Edited and contributed to the broader Ballard Brief publication series

Outcomes

  • Published two research articles each drawing on 200+ hours of independent research
  • Contributed to a growing library of accessible social issue analysis used by students and practitioners
