Strategy Research

Workforce Development Plan

Cicero Group · Salt Lake City, UT · 2021

Overview

As a Senior Business Analyst at Cicero Group, led the research and synthesis for an equity-oriented workforce development plan for a client. The project involved evaluating 100+ potential interventions across workforce training, education pathways, and economic mobility programs, producing strategic recommendations grounded in evidence and feasibility.

Approach

  • Conducted a landscape analysis of 100+ workforce development interventions across sectors
  • Assessed each intervention against criteria including evidence base, equity orientation, and implementation feasibility
  • Synthesized findings into a prioritized set of strategic recommendations for the client
  • Managed competing deadlines alongside concurrent client engagements across nonprofits, government, and the private sector

Outcomes

  • Delivered an evidence-based workforce development plan centered on equity
  • Provided the client with a prioritized intervention framework for strategic decision-making
