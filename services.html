Services

I support organizations with measurement, strategy, and operational infrastructure. Facilitation, communication, and research are incorporated across all areas of my work. Whether I'm designing a strategic plan or drafting a report for stakeholders, I bring the same commitment to prioritizing relationships, making complexity legible, and incorporating rigorous evidence.

01

Measurement & Evaluation

Helping organizations design rigorous approaches to understand and act on their impact.

Spanning the strategic questions of what to measure, a theory of change that connects the dots, the technical infrastructure to capture it, and the communications that make data legible and useful for diverse audiences.

  • Design of theory of change, logic models, and results frameworks
  • Quantitative and qualitative data collection and analysis
  • Equity-centred evaluation design focused on structural disparities
  • Data system design and implementation (including Salesforce)
  • Impact reporting and stakeholder communication
02

Strategy & Program Design

Supporting organizations to clarify direction, design programs that work, and build the partnerships and operational systems to make them sustainable.

From strategic thinking to hands-on implementation capacity – going beyond planning to iterative implementation, institutionalized sustainability, and continuous improvement.

  • Multi-year strategic planning and facilitation
  • Stakeholder alignment across departments and partnerships
  • Governance and organisational design processes
  • Collective impact strategy and backbone support
  • Workshop and training design and delivery
03

Operations & Project Management

Building the operational infrastructure that makes good work sustainable, from data systems and governance protocols to end-to-end project delivery.

Managing timelines, coordinating across teams, and designing workflows so that strategy doesn't stall at implementation.

  • Data system design and implementation (including Salesforce)
  • Workflow design, documentation, and process improvement
  • End-to-end project management across concurrent engagements
  • Team supervision, coaching, and performance management
  • Data governance protocols and compliance infrastructure
