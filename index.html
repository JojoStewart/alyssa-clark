About

I hold an MSc in Human Rights from LSE and an MPA from Brigham Young University. Before consulting independently, I led data strategy at Promise Partnership Utah — a cradle-to-career collective impact partnership — and delivered concurrent client engagements at Cicero Group, a boutique strategy consultancy.

I've presented at national conferences, contributed to sector publications, and built networks across the collective impact and racial equity fields. I'm based in London and available for project-based and ongoing engagements.
