My work sits at the intersection of rigorous analysis and relational trust — I'm as comfortable building a Salesforce database as I am facilitating a room full of stakeholders toward shared goals. I hold an MSc in Human Rights from LSE, an MPA from Brigham Young University, and I'm based in London.
